Some of the Latest Projects I Worked On

Corporate Identity
for a Jewelry Brand

Logo Creation and Brand Identity for My Moon. My Moon is a Ukrainian jewelry brand. the production of which is located in Bali island. The logo has several meanings. Firstly, two moons as two “O” letters. Also, the waxing moon symbolizes youth, while the waning one - mature age; thus, the jewelry is suitable for girls and women of any age. Finally, two moons make up a full lunar cycle, which is a symbol of harmony and abundance.
